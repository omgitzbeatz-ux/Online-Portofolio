Faceless

Director & Editor
← Back to Projects
Logline
Consumed by a past they refuse to let go of, a paranoid protagonist slowly loses their grip on reality as their darkest, most obsessive fears begin to violently manifest in their daily life.
Synopsis

The film open by a voice we hear calling the name “idir” and than we get to see the protagonist wake up to and the phone rings and idir picks up to hear his friend calling him to say his condolences but idir was ignorant.  Than we see idir go to the dinner table and after a certain amount of event he start to feel paranoid and starts seeing these faceless creatures that represent his grief and him failing to move on/ let go.

Statement of Intent

The reason to why I wanted to make this film was because I came to realise a lot of people tend to struggle with grief and to let go of certain aspects of life and I am also a victim to these traits. After having a clear vision of what I wanted to film and the idea of the film I started to think I need a way to portray the grief a person is going through and from my own experience how it felt and so I looked at my old photos that I have taken and I found an image labelled “Suppressed” and that is when it hit me the faceless creature picture that sitirred everything in me and I wanted to make this static image, dynamic.
