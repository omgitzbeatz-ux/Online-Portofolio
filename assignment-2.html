Logline
Heart of Ifrane is meant to show how the town center keeps everyone connected, even as the years pass. The film is dark for the sole reason that I want the audience to hear rather than see.
Synopsis

A film that was made mainly for the audience to focus on listening and the dark visuals that represent late night Ifrane. Trying to show that people of all age groups spend their time in these places in Ifrane, which makes it the heart.

The Journey

To start, when I was given this assignment, the first thing I thought of was showing the heart of a city. I spend most of my time in Ifrane, so I decided to capture the feeling of downtown. Downtown is the heart of Ifrane, and I wanted the audience to feel that, mostly through sound. I was also inspired by a movie that used a lens blur effect. I loved how it looked, so I learned how to do it. I am really proud of how it turned out.
