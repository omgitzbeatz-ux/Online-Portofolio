Logline
Trapped in an endless, purely visual dreamscape by a single press of the snooze button.
Synopsis

A purely visual short film where a simple press of the snooze button traps an ordinary person in an infinite dream state, choosing eternal sleep over facing the waking world.

The Journey

As this was my first assignment I had no idea how to approach it and I was quite lost so I just went with what usually happens to me when I try to wake up for class. This process taught me that the process to find an idea is easy if you don’t think to far ahead just look for what I relate to and it will all work out eventually.
