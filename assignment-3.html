Logline
A paranoid young man, driven by a twisted sense of family duty, harshly scolds his sister over a simple phone call.
Synopsis

Film starts by the sister taking a phone call and than the twisted brother starts to scold her sister in a toxic manner which breaks out to a fight.

The Journey

After finishing my kitchen scene for my final film I asked my cast if it would be possible that I borrowed them for 10 more minutes for a relief session where I asked them to release their frustration and I told them to act out a role of a brother and sister that have a toxic relationship.
